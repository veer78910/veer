Stop Bullying

What is bullying?

Bullying is the repeated and intentional use of words or actions to hurt, intimidate, or humiliate another person.

The effects of bullying

Bullying can have serious and long-lasting effects on a person's mental and emotional well-being. It can lead to anxiety, depression, low self-esteem, and even suicidal thoughts or behaviors.

How to prevent bullying

  • Promote kindness and respect
  • Encourage bystander intervention
  • Foster open communication
